FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you make gluten-free pasta?

Yes, we offer gluten-free options. Please let our staff know when placing your order.

Do I have to book, or can I just show up?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking ahead during peak hours.

How do I book a table?

You can book a table through our website, social media pages, or by calling us directly.

Do you offer takeaway or delivery?

Yes! We offer both takeaway and home delivery services.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Sunday from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Do you cater for events or parties?

Yes, we provide catering services for birthdays, weddings, and corporate events.

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Absolutely. We have a variety of vegetarian and vegan meals available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, and POS payments.

Can I customize my order?

Yes, you can customize your meals to suit your preferences, subject to availability.

Are your ingredients fresh?

Yes, we use fresh, high-quality ingredients prepared daily.
